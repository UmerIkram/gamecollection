FIFA 16

I love soccer. So it's obvious that FIFA 16 would be one of my favourite games. I have to say it was a bit of a disappointment. There was so much hype around it (which I guess is normal) but I expected it to be better than FIFA 15 but the only real improvement was the graphics. I mean playing with the women was interesting, but I never really got into it myself personally.
