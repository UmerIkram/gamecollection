Titanfall

This IS the first game I've ever played. I got Titanfall in a bundle with my new Xbox One. This is all I ever played for a good year. I have to say I really enjoyed it, the graphics blew me away. This could be because I only ever had the original Xbox before, but I decided it was smarter to wait and buy the next gen console. This game was not disappointing at all. I absolutely loved it.
